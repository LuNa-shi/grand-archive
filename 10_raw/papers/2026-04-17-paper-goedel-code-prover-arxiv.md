## Abstract-level observations
- The paper targets automated code verification in Lean 4.
- It proposes hierarchical proof search that decomposes complex verification goals into simpler subgoals before tactic-level proving.
- A decomposition score is used both for training reward and inference-time ranking.
- The system trains an 8B model for both decomposition and completion.
- The method combines supervised initialization with hybrid reinforcement learning.
- Reported result: 62.0% prove success on three Lean-based code verification benchmarks with 427 tasks.
- The paper claims a 2.6x improvement over the strongest baseline and better efficiency than much larger neural provers.
