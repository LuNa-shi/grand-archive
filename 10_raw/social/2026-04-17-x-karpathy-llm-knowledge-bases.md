## Captured text summary
Karpathy describes a workflow where LLMs build personal knowledge bases around research interests. He outlines:
- ingesting source documents into a raw/ directory
- incrementally compiling a wiki as markdown files
- using Obsidian as the viewing frontend
- asking complex questions against the wiki once it grows
- generating outputs as markdown, slides, or figures
- filing outputs back into the wiki so explorations accumulate
- running health checks / linting over the wiki
- adding auxiliary tools like search engines for larger queries
- considering synthetic data generation and finetuning as the knowledge base scales
