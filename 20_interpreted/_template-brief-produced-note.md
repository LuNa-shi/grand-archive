# Produced Note Template

Use this for concise source interpretations.

## What this source is saying
One short paragraph on the main message.

## Why it exists
- what problem it is trying to solve
- why this problem matters

## What it actually does
- 2 to 4 bullets on the real method, system, or move
- focus on the core mechanism, not every new term

## Main figure / picture to remember
- include the most important figure, architecture image, or screenshot when available
- explain only what the picture proves or clarifies

## What was actually validated
- what they tested or demonstrated
- 1 to 3 bullets only
- focus on whether the claimed idea is supported

## Why it matters to me
- connect it to the user's interests in a few bullets

## Open questions
- concise questions worth tracking later
